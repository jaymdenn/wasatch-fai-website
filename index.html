Is This Right For You?

See If You're a Candidate

Our procedure is designed for a specific type of chronic heel pain. Here's how to know if it might help you.

You May Be a Great Fit If...

  • You've had heel pain for 6 months or longer
  • You've tried PT, injections, or orthotics without lasting relief
  • Your pain is worst with the first steps in the morning
  • You want to avoid hospital surgery and long recovery
  • You're ready to finally fix this problem for good

You May Need Evaluation First If...

  • Your pain started recently (less than 3 months)
  • You have numbness, tingling, or burning in your foot
  • You haven't tried any conservative treatment yet
  • Your pain may be nerve-related rather than fascia

Frequently Asked Questions

Traditional surgery requires a hospital, general anesthesia, larger incisions with stitches, and weeks of non-weightbearing recovery. Our ultrasound-guided micro-release is done in-office with local anesthesia, requires no stitches, and you walk out the same day.

After 6+ months, plantar fasciitis often becomes a degenerative condition rather than inflammatory. The fascia becomes chronically thickened and damaged. Treatments targeting inflammation provide only temporary relief because the underlying structural problem remains.

You walk out the same day. Most patients return to daily activities within 1-2 weeks and resume full activity including exercise within 4-6 weeks. We provide a recovery kit and detailed instructions, plus two follow-up visits are included.

Our transparent pricing eliminates insurance bureaucracy, surprise bills, and authorization delays. You know exactly what you'll pay upfront. For many patients, our pricing is comparable to or less than what they'd pay through insurance for hospital-based surgery.
